Hi, my name is Kevin. I consider refinishing furniture hands-on therapy for life. I strive to provide a quality product at an affordable price. I find broken down pieces and restore them to their natural beauty. Upon request, I can do the same for a piece of furniture you want restored as well!

All pieces are stripped, sanded, glued (if necessary), stained and a finish sprayed applied to them. I use a low luster finish to avoid the shiny, plastic look that detracts from the wood. I then do a final sanding with a high grit sand paper and a rub out with Orange Glow.
